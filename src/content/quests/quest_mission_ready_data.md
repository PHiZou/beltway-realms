## Objective
Deliver decision-ready reporting where errors are expensive and ambiguity gets questioned.

## Context
In the Citadel, you do not ship “pretty charts.” You ship trusted truth under scrutiny.

## Approach
- Define the metric contract: what each number means, how it’s computed, and why it matters.
- Build repeatable pipelines for refresh and validation.
- Add QA checkpoints: anomaly checks, totals reconciliation, and source-to-output trace paths.

## Challenges
- Conflicting definitions across stakeholders.
- Edge cases that only appear at scale.

## Outcome
A reliable reporting layer leaders can use without second-guessing the data.

## Next Upgrade
Formalize data tests and lineage so trust becomes automatic, not heroic.
