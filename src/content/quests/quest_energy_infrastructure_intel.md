## Objective
Map the constraints behind AI growth: power, transformers, interconnect queues, and data center expansion across Northern Virginia.

## Context
Ashburn does not run on hype. It runs on megawatts. If power delivery lags, compute stalls, and the whole realm feels it.

## Approach
- Gather public signals: utility filings, interconnection data, data center announcements, and regional infrastructure reports.
- Normalize sources into a single “truth layer” with clear provenance.
- Publish an explorable view: regions, constraints, and implications.

## Key Decisions
- Prioritize traceability over flash: every claim must tie back to a source.
- Model the system as a network of bottlenecks, not a linear pipeline.

## Outcome
A living intelligence brief that connects AI infrastructure demand to real-world electrical constraints.

Add automated ingestion + change detection so the Dominion map updates when new filings or announcements land.
