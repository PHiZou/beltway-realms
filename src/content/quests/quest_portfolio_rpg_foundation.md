## Objective
Transform a standard portfolio into an RPG interface that makes technical work memorable.

## Context
Most portfolios are catalogs. This becomes a world: quests, skills, regions, bosses.

## Approach
Build three core screens first:
1) Start Screen
2) Character Sheet (resume)
3) Skill Tree (capabilities)

Store progress locally (visited regions, quests viewed, XP).

## Outcome
A portfolio experience that communicates systems thinking through interaction.
